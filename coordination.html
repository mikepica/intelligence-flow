Composable Intelligence

From Personal Expertise to Coordinated Intelligence

Every person's domain knowledge becomes a repeatable AI skill — personal and shared. Skills compose into collaboration workflows across people and teams. AI optimizes the coordination. The scorecard database keeps everything goal-aligned and adapts the goals based on what the system learns.

Personal Skills encode unique expertise

Each person's domain knowledge — data pipelines, validation criteria, analytical frameworks — is codified as a repeatable AI skill. A researcher's compound analysis method becomes executable. Their unique contribution is captured and composable.

Collaboration becomes the workflow

When skills need to compose across people, the collaboration itself is codified. The handoff from compound analysis to biomarker validation isn't ad-hoc — it's a repeatable, versioned, optimizable workflow. Teams develop shared skill methodologies for their coordination patterns.

AI optimizes the coordination itself

AI observes every workflow execution. It identifies bottlenecks, surfaces collaboration opportunities across therapeutic areas, and suggests coordination patterns — different team compositions, novel skill chains, handoff protocol improvements — that humans wouldn't think to try.

Scorecard steers and adapts goals

The scorecard database doesn't just track progress — it steers collaboration toward organizational goals. As skill outputs accumulate, the system recommends adaptations to the goals themselves based on what the collaboration reveals. The goals evolve with the intelligence.

What AI-optimized coordination surfaces

"Compound X's biomarker profile matches an active immunology program — recommend cross-TA workflow with immunology DS's personal skill"
"Clinical feasibility workflow runs 40% faster when translational validation uses updated stratification criteria — suggest workflow adaptation"
"Portfolio review goal should expand to include 3 candidates that emerged from market access modeling — recommend goal modification"
